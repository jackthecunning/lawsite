bio: Caryn Steiger is a distinguished estate planning attorney who combines legal expertise with accounting credentials to provide comprehensive wealth transfer and tax planning services to high-net-worth individuals and families. As both a licensed attorney and CPA with an LL.M. in Taxation, Steiger offers sophisticated estate planning strategies that minimize tax liability while achieving clients' personal and philanthropic objectives. Her practice encompasses the preparation of complex wills and trust documents, business succession planning, and charitable giving strategies. Steiger has extensive experience with generation-skipping trusts, grantor retained annuity trusts, and other advanced estate planning techniques. She regularly advises clients on estate and gift tax matters, including annual exclusion planning and lifetime exemption strategies. Steiger also handles estate and trust administration, representing executors and trustees in complex probate and trust administration matters. Her accounting background enables her to work effectively with clients' financial advisors and provide comprehensive tax planning advice. Steiger is known for her ability to translate complex tax and legal concepts into understandable terms and for developing creative solutions to challenging estate planning objectives. She frequently speaks at professional conferences and contributes to publications on estate planning and taxation topics.
