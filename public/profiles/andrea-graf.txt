bio: Andrea Graf is a passionate advocate for employee rights and civil liberties, with extensive experience representing individuals in employment discrimination, harassment, and wrongful termination cases. As a Partner at Swartz Campbell, Andrea has successfully litigated high-profile cases involving workplace discrimination based on gender, race, age, and disability. Her background in women's studies from Barnard College informs her deep understanding of systemic workplace inequalities and drives her commitment to achieving justice for her clients. Andrea regularly handles complex employment law matters including wage and hour disputes, non-compete agreements, and civil rights violations. She has been recognized as a Rising Star by Super Lawyers and serves on the board of the Pennsylvania Employment Lawyers Association, where she works to advance employee protection legislation.
