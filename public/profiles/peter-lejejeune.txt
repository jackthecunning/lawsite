bio: Peter Le Jejeune is a sophisticated securities attorney who counsels public and private companies, investment advisers, and financial institutions on complex securities law matters and corporate compliance issues. With dual J.D. and M.B.A. degrees and active securities licenses, Peter combines deep legal knowledge with practical business acumen to provide strategic advice on securities offerings, mergers and acquisitions, and regulatory compliance. His practice encompasses public offerings, private placements, and exempt transactions under federal and state securities laws. Peter regularly advises clients on SEC reporting requirements, insider trading compliance, and corporate governance matters. He has extensive experience with investment adviser regulation, including registration requirements, compliance programs, and regulatory examinations. Peter also handles securities litigation and enforcement matters, representing clients in SEC investigations and FINRA disciplinary proceedings. His finance background and securities licenses allow him to understand complex financial products and market structures, making him particularly effective in representing clients in the rapidly evolving fintech and cryptocurrency sectors.
