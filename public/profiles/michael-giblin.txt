bio: With over 25 years of experience in corporate and real estate law, Michael E. Giblin serves as Managing Partner of Swartz Campbell. His expertise spans complex commercial transactions, real estate development, and corporate governance. Michael has represented Fortune 500 companies, real estate developers, and emerging businesses throughout his distinguished career.

Prior to joining Swartz Campbell, Michael worked at several prestigious law firms in Philadelphia and New York. He earned his J.D. and M.B.A. simultaneously from the University of Pennsylvania, graduating magna cum laude from both programs. His dual background in law and business provides clients with comprehensive strategic counsel.

Michael is actively involved in the legal community, serving on the board of the Philadelphia Bar Association and participating in various professional organizations. He regularly speaks at legal conferences and has authored numerous articles on corporate law and real estate transactions.
