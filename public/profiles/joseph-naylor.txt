bio: Joseph Naylor is a dedicated personal injury and workers' compensation attorney who fights tirelessly for injured workers and accident victims throughout Pennsylvania. With a background in kinesiology, Joseph possesses a unique understanding of human movement, injury mechanisms, and rehabilitation processes that proves invaluable in personal injury cases. His education in kinesiology allows him to work effectively with medical professionals, understand injury causation, and advocate for appropriate medical treatment and compensation for his clients. Joseph's practice focuses on workplace injuries, construction accidents, automotive collisions, and premises liability cases. He is certified in workers' compensation law and has extensive experience navigating the complex Pennsylvania workers' compensation system. Joseph regularly represents injured workers in hearings before workers' compensation judges and appeals to commonwealth court. He is passionate about holding employers and insurance companies accountable for providing proper benefits to injured workers. Joseph's compassionate approach and thorough case preparation have resulted in substantial recoveries for clients facing medical bills, lost wages, and permanent disabilities. He is committed to ensuring that injured individuals receive the full compensation they deserve for their injuries and losses.
