bio: Stephen Plonski is a unique employment attorney who combines legal expertise with professional human resources credentials to provide comprehensive employment law services to employers and employees. As both a licensed attorney and PHR-certified HR professional, Plonski understands employment issues from both legal and practical human resources perspectives. His practice encompasses employment discrimination litigation, wage and hour compliance, workplace harassment investigations, and HR policy development. Plonski regularly represents employers in EEOC charges, Department of Labor investigations, and employment-related litigation. He also provides training to management teams on employment law compliance, harassment prevention, and best practices for hiring, discipline, and termination decisions. His HR background enables him to draft comprehensive employee handbooks, develop compliant compensation structures, and design effective performance management systems. Plonski has particular expertise in multi-state employment law compliance for companies with geographically dispersed workforces. He is an active member of the Society for Human Resource Management and regularly speaks at HR conferences on employment law developments and compliance strategies. His dual qualifications make him an invaluable resource for companies seeking to minimize employment law risks while maintaining effective HR practices.
