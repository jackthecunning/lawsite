bio: Paige Fuchion is a distinguished immigration attorney who helps individuals, families, and businesses navigate the complex U.S. immigration system. With fluency in Spanish and French and extensive experience in international business transactions, Paige serves a diverse clientele including multinational corporations, entrepreneurs, and immigrants seeking permanent residence or citizenship. Her practice encompasses all aspects of immigration law, from family-based petitions and asylum cases to employment-based visas and naturalization proceedings. Paige has particular expertise in business immigration, including H-1B, L-1, and EB-5 investor visas. She regularly assists companies with I-9 compliance, workforce immigration strategies, and immigration aspects of corporate transactions. Her international relations background and multilingual abilities allow her to effectively represent clients from various cultural and linguistic backgrounds. Paige is committed to staying current with rapidly changing immigration policies and provides clients with strategic advice to achieve their immigration goals while maintaining compliance with federal regulations.
