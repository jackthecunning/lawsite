bio: Robert Goldman is a dual-qualified attorney and CPA who provides sophisticated tax planning and business structuring advice to high-net-worth individuals, closely held businesses, and multinational corporations. With an LL.M. in Taxation from NYU and a strong accounting background from Wharton, Robert offers clients comprehensive tax solutions that span federal, state, and international tax issues. His practice encompasses tax-efficient business formations, mergers and acquisitions, estate and gift tax planning, and IRS controversy work. Robert regularly represents clients before the IRS in audits, appeals, and collection matters. He has extensive experience with complex tax structures including partnerships, LLCs, S-corporations, and international entities. Robert is a member of the American College of Tax Counsel, a prestigious organization limited to the top tax attorneys in the United States. He frequently speaks at tax conferences and contributes to leading tax publications.
