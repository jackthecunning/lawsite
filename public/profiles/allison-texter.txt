bio: Allison Texter is a healthcare attorney who specializes in healthcare privacy law, regulatory compliance, and healthcare business transactions. With a background in health sciences and specialized certification in healthcare privacy and security, Texter provides comprehensive legal counsel to healthcare providers, health systems, and healthcare technology companies. Her practice focuses on HIPAA compliance, health information technology law, and healthcare data security matters. Texter regularly advises clients on privacy policies, business associate agreements, and breach response protocols. She has extensive experience with healthcare mergers and acquisitions, physician practice transactions, and healthcare joint ventures. Texter also handles regulatory compliance matters including Medicare and Medicaid reimbursement issues, Stark Law compliance, and Anti-Kickback Statute matters. Her health sciences background provides valuable insight into clinical operations and helps her develop practical compliance solutions that work in real-world healthcare settings. Texter stays current with rapidly evolving healthcare technology regulations, including telemedicine compliance, digital health applications, and artificial intelligence in healthcare. She is an active member of the American Health Lawyers Association and regularly attends healthcare law conferences to stay abreast of industry developments and regulatory changes affecting healthcare providers.
