Medical Malpractice Settlement Reached in Lemons v. Meguerian

Swartz Campbell has successfully negotiated a confidential settlement in the medical malpractice case of Lemons v. Meguerian, representing a family whose loved one suffered complications following a surgical procedure.

The case involved allegations of surgical negligence and failure to provide adequate post-operative care. After extensive discovery and consultation with medical experts, the firm was able to demonstrate that the standard of care was not met, leading to preventable complications.

Attorney Michael Torres, who handled the case, worked closely with leading medical experts to build a compelling case. "Medical malpractice cases require meticulous attention to detail and the ability to translate complex medical issues for judges and juries," Torres explained.

The settlement provides financial security for the family while avoiding the emotional toll of a lengthy trial. The terms of the settlement remain confidential at the family's request.

This case adds to the firm's track record in medical malpractice litigation, where we have recovered millions of dollars for clients who have suffered due to medical negligence. Our attorneys work with top medical experts nationwide to ensure our clients receive the compensation they deserve.
